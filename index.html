I’m a junior front end developer specialising in JavaScript. I have been interested in technology since I was young though growing up in a post communistic state where it was very unusual for women to study technical subjects it never seemed like an option to study Computing. So I learnt the standard human languages. I speak Czech, German, English, Italian, Russian and language of hypnosis. When I arrived to London and entered world on start ups as a project manager it became clear to me that programming was a skill I wanted to gain. So in February 2019 I began my journey to become a developer. In June 2019 I signed up for bachelors degree in Computing with focus on web development with Open University which I’m studying part time and started a work experience for a start up called Skylight as a front end developer.

The moment when I wrote my first code I knew that I was destined to be a programmer. I enjoy the blend of logical and creative skills, the ability to explore, learn and get better. I love working with JavaScript and React building apps. I am curious, enjoy to create. I can spend hours doing puzzles.

Fun Fact: I am in Spiderman: Far From Home talking to Spiderman in Czech giving him Tony Stark’s glasses:)
